# Netflix Business Insights Report

## Executive Summary

This report presents key findings from the Netflix Content Strategy Analytics project.

The analysis explores Netflix’s content portfolio to understand distribution patterns across content types, countries, genres, ratings, and release timelines.

## Key Findings

- Movies dominate the overall content library
- TV Shows present strong retention and engagement potential
- Content production is concentrated in a few major countries
- A limited set of genres dominates the catalog
- Netflix has expanded content rapidly over time
- Strategic opportunities exist in regional expansion and genre diversification

## Business Implications

The findings suggest that Netflix can strengthen its content strategy by:

- Increasing high-retention TV content
- Expanding regional representation
- Improving genre balance
- Aligning ratings and content categories with audience segments

## Conclusion

This project shows how data analytics can support strategic decision-making in the streaming and entertainment industry by converting raw content metadata into actionable business insights.
