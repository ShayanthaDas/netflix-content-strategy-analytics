# Business Problem

Netflix operates in a highly competitive streaming market where content decisions directly impact audience growth, retention, and engagement.

The key business challenge is to understand:

- Which type of content dominates the platform
- Which countries contribute the most content
- What genres are most common
- How Netflix’s content library has changed over time
- What strategic opportunities exist for future content planning

This project analyzes Netflix content data to generate actionable business insights and support content strategy decisions.
