# Business Recommendations

## Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Expand high-performing TV content
Netflix should continue investing in TV Shows and series-based content to improve viewer retention and platform engagement.

### 2. Strengthen regional content strategy
Netflix can increase global audience appeal by expanding content production and acquisition in underrepresented regions.

### 3. Diversify genre portfolio
Reducing overdependence on a few dominant genres can help Netflix improve audience reach and content variety.

### 4. Use ratings strategically for audience targeting
Rating patterns can help improve content positioning for different audience segments.

### 5. Focus on strategic value, not only content volume
Content planning should prioritize engagement, retention, and long-term business value rather than only library size.
